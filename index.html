About Services FAQ


Su Alterations, in the Woodland Mall, is a family-owned business that has been in operation for over 30 years. We provide quick, quality work on all kinds of garments. Please call (616) 975-7877 for any inquiries regarding alterations and garments. For inquries on pricing, please bring items in-store.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Are appointments needed? Appointments are not needed.

  2. How long does an alteration take? Alteration time depends on the garment and type of alterations.

  3. What is the cost for an alteration? The cost depends on the type of garment and alteration. Please bring in the garment in-store for a free quote.

  4. Do you provide measurements? We do not provide services for measurements.

  5. Do you do embroidery? We do not provide services for embroidery.

  6. Are heels required when trying on my dress? If you’re wearing any sort of heels with your dress, then they’re required when trying on your garment.

  7. When are your busiest seasons? April - May. Please bring in any prom or bridal dresses as early as possible, or at least a week before the event.

  8. Do you do custom projects? All custom projects must be seen by Su (Head Seamstress) and only during December and January.

  9. Do I have to pay up front? Payment is required on the drop-off of garments.

  10. Where is the store entrance? Su Alterations is located inside Woodland Mall, near Macy’s. There is an outdoor mall entrance directly outside Su Alterations’ main entrance, making it the quickest way to enter. Please refer to the picture below for reference.
