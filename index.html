Frequently Asked Questions

Are appointments required?

No appointments are needed.

How long do alterations take?

Timing depends on the garment and alteration type.

How much do alterations cost?

Do you provide measurements?

No measurement services are provided.

Do you do embroidery?

No embroidery services.

Are heels required for dress fittings?

If you will wear heels with your dress, please bring them.

When are your busiest seasons?

April–May (prom and wedding season).

Do you do custom projects?

Custom projects must be approved by Su during December or January.
